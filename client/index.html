Rules of the game

Welcome to Through the Ages!

You will be given a question and 4 possible answers.
You have 3 lives.
If you get a question wrong, you lose a life.
If you get a question right, you move on to the next question.
If you lose all 3 lives, you lose the game.
If you get all 10 questions right, you win the game.

Good luck!
